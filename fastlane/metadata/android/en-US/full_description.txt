NotiLogger is a lightweight, open-source tool designed to help you capture and manage your system notifications locally.

Key Features:
- Auto-Logging: Saves notifications to an encrypted SQLite database.
- Advanced Filters: Blacklist or Whitelist apps and block specific keywords.
- Telegram Forwarding: Send notifications to multiple Telegram bots and chats in real-time.
- Privacy First: Data stays on your device. SQLCipher AES-256 encryption included.
- Backup & Restore: Export and import logs via JSON.
- Smart Search: Easily search through archived notifications.
- App Lock: Secure your logs with a PIN or Password.
